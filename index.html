Golden Rabbit

This mod introduces the Golden Rabbit critter that can spawn in the same biome as the regular Rabbit, but it behaves as the Leprechaun (as in it runs aways when it sees the player). It drops a few coins as a suggestion that it will reward the player if you _*ahem*_ -- capture it. - It drops 10 Gold Coins on Adventurer Mode and 25 Gold Coins on Survivor Mode.
